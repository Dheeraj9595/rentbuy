#Cloths

GET	/api/admin/cloths/	List all clothes
POST	/api/admin/cloths/	Create a new cloth
GET	/api/admin/cloths/{id}/	Retrieve a cloth item
PUT	/api/admin/cloths/{id}/	Update a cloth item
DELETE	/api/admin/cloths/{id}/	Delete a cloth item
POST	/api/admin/cloths/{id}/approve/	Approve a cloth
POST	/api/admin/cloths/{id}/reject/	Reject a cloth
